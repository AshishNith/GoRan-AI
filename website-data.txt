--- Case Study 1 ---
URL slug:             ibs-brokers
Client name:          IBS Insurance Brokers
Tag:                  Workflow Automation
Headline:             How we saved IBS Brokers 1,280 hours of manual data entry every single month
Timeline:             28 Days
Impact:               1,280 Hours Saved / Month
Services used:        AI Workflow Audit, Custom Document Pipeline, API Integration
Tech stack:           Python, FastAPI, Airtable, Google Cloud Document AI, Slack API

Challenge paragraph:  IBS Brokers processed thousands of insurance claims weekly, but the data entry workflow was entirely manual. Support staff spent entire shifts copying information from PDFs and emails into their claims database. Errors were common, processing time averaged 12 minutes per claim, and the backlog grew faster than their team could handle.
Discovery paragraph:  We embedded with their operations team for one week, mapping every step of the claims journey. The bottleneck was immediately clear: claims arriving in multiple formats (email attachments, scanned PDFs, web forms) all required human interpretation before they could enter the system. Each format needed different handling logic.
Solution paragraph:   We deployed a multi-agent pipeline: one agent classifies incoming documents, a second extracts structured fields using vision-language models, and a third validates confidence scores before writing to the database. Low-confidence claims are flagged for human review. The entire process runs unsupervised on a 2-minute cron cycle.
Impact paragraph:     Data entry time dropped from 12 minutes to 90 seconds per claim. The backlog cleared within the first week. Staff were reassigned to higher-value tasks like client relationship management. IBS reported a 40% reduction in processing errors and estimated £180k annual savings in operational costs.

Impact paragraph:     The agent handles 200+ calls per day. No-shows dropped 73% thanks to automated reminders. Front desk staff now focus on patient care instead of phone management. The clinic booked 88 new patients in the first month and reported a 12% increase in overall monthly revenue.

  17. FAQ — HOME PAGE
================================================================================

Q1: What exactly is an AI Agent, and how is it different from a chatbot?
A1: An AI Agent is an autonomous system that perceives its environment, makes decisions, and takes actions to achieve specific goals. Unlike chatbots that only respond to prompts, agents execute multi-step workflows — they can query databases, send emails, update CRMs, escalate issues, and learn from outcomes without human intervention.

Q2: How long does a typical AI Agent implementation take?
A2: Most implementations take 8-14 weeks from kickoff to production. The timeline depends on complexity, number of integrations, and data readiness. We start with a discovery phase to scope accurately and identify quick wins that can be delivered sooner.

Q3: Do I need a technical team to maintain the agent after deployment?
A3: No — we design agents to be self-maintaining with clear dashboards, automatic error handling, and alerting. We provide a full handover session and documentation, and offer ongoing support packages if you'd like us to manage updates and improvements.

Q4: What systems can your agents integrate with?
A4: Our agents integrate with virtually any system that has an API — CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), communication tools (Slack, Teams, Gmail), databases (PostgreSQL, Airtable, MongoDB), and custom internal tools. We also handle legacy systems without APIs through middleware adapters.

Q5: How do you handle data security and privacy?
A5: Security is built into every layer of our deployments. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow SOC2-aligned practices, provide audit logs, and sign NDAs and DPAs. Agents process data within your environment — we never store or retain sensitive information on our servers.

Q6: What happens if the agent makes a mistake?
A6: Every agent includes human-in-the-loop checks for high-stakes decisions, confidence thresholds that flag uncertainty, and detailed audit trails for every action. If an error occurs, the agent logs it, alerts your team, and can roll back the action automatically. We also provide monitoring dashboards for full visibility.
